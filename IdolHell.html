IDOL HELL

This is a project for a photography class with the topic of replicating an existing photographer’s style, which in this case Ken Lum was the inspiration.
Main subject(s) of the piece are just stuffed plushies from an animation show called Love Live and is the driving force for plunging the person aka me in question into an endless rabbit hole called “Idol Hell.”
Posters were combined with humorous text/fonts and studio setting photographs.
