ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Daniel Tran. I am a Treaty 1 Territory (or Winnipeg, Manitoba) based graphic designer on a mission to create unique designs while maintaining various design ethics and standards. My interest in design began in my late days of high school when I was inspired by the aesthetics of minimalistic graphic design. Naturally, I had no idea what I was doing when it came to designing something but overtime improvement would come.
Majority of my projects contains a very satire feeling to it, mixed with a combination of the standard graphic design standards. Why satire? Satire is an interesting and funny concept that I really enjoy, perhaps due to internet memes and it built in a personality trait in me. I also enjoy doing professional and work like creating email materials, graphic assets, and posters for student groups and companies.
My next goal as a designer is exploring a more unique and expressive style because I feel like I have a decent understanding of design in general but I would like to go even further beyond in the expressive and contemporary area.
